About Us

At "The Lunchbox," we don’t just serve meals; we offer an experience that blends the deliciousness of Thai flavors with the rich traditions of the country. We take pride in sourcing fresh, locally-grown ingredients, and each dish we prepare reflects the diversity of authentic Thai flavors, filled with history and culture.

How it works

1. Pick a Night

Every week, we bring fresh and delicious meals to your doorstep from across Thailand, ready to heat and enjoy at your convenience.

2. Choose your sides

Select from a range of authentic sides such as spicy pickles, crunchy snacks, and flavorful dipping sauces. Customize your weekly menu and swap out sides anytime to suit your cravings.

03. Heat, Eat & Repeat

Your meal arrives ready to heat in just 30 minutes—no cooking or prep required. After enjoying your meal, we’ll pick up your empty tins during the next delivery. It’s easy, sustainable, and hassle-free!
